# Security Policy

Memeri's connector runs on your machine and its agents act with your user's
permissions — we take reports about it seriously.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **security@memeri.ai** (or see [memeri.ai/security](https://memeri.ai/security)) with:
- What you found and where (this repo, the installer flow, the hosted service)
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact as you understand it

Please don't open public issues or PRs for vulnerabilities.

We aim to acknowledge reports within a few days. Good-faith research conducted
without service disruption or access to other users' data will not be pursued
legally.

The hosted service's full responsible-disclosure policy lives at
memeri.ai/security.
